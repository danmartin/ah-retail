About The Animal House

The Animal House is the premier destination for all of your healthy cat and dog needs. Within our store you will find a large variety of all-natural pet foods,supplements, collars & leads, toys, supplies, and unique goods you won’t find in other stores.

Our self-service wash facility is like no other you’ve experienced. Professionally designed to put convenience and fun into the “chore” of washing your dog, The Animal House will give you an experience fit for a canine king or queen. Just bring in your dirty dog and we will provide you with everything you need to brush, wash and dry them. We even clean up after you! We also offer professional, full service grooming by appointment.

At The Animal House, we understand that your cat or dog is much more than just a pet, they are a cherished member of the family. We are delighted to offer you a place where you can wash, feed, outfit and love your pet.
